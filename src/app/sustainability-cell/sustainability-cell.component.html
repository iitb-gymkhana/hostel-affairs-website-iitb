Sustainability’ or ‘sustainable development’, a relatively new concept, is about providing for our current needs without putting at risk our future generations, by striving for enviro-socio-economic development. It is about working towards prosperity and at the same time protecting our planet. As a premier institute in the nation and an Institute of Eminence, we aim to be role models for the rest of the nation into a sustainable future.

Sustainability is a way of life to be achieved by a combination of awareness, education, research, innovation and policy and Sustainability Cell will work alongside the campus community to make IITB a beacon of sustainability.

We aim to:
Spread awareness about sustainability amongst the campus community. Usher in technological innovations in the institute and coordinate with various institute bodies, faculty and the student community to integrate sustainable technologies into our institute.

Make the campus a test bed for pilot projects, involving professors and students Create a platform in the institute for discussion and debates regarding various sustainability issues on and off campus

The Sustainability Cell brings regards from a sustainable future waiting ahead.
