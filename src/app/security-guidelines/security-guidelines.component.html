  3. Stay away from the lakeside area after dark and do not attempt to climb the hill as well. This is for your own safety, from animals and from falls that can result in grievous injury. There are penalties for violating these rules.

Over the past year, quite a few laptops/mobiles have been stolen/lost/misplaced. Students need to be especially vigilant and careful. Such incidences have happened at all times of day and night.

  1. NEVER GO OUT LEAVING YOUR ROOM UNLOCKED. If you have a room-mate who is in the habit of doing so, impress upon him or her that this is an inconvenience you cannot afford.
  2. WHEN YOU GO TO SLEEP LATCH YOUR ROOM. Recently a laptop was stolen when a student was sleeping in the room. Set up a schedule with your roommate so that he or she does not have to wake you up at ungodly hours.
  3. IF SOMETHING IS LOST / STOLEN / MISPLACED REPORT FIRST TO SECURITY. They will help you file a proper police complaint if required. There are certain categories of complaints, under which, even if you find your lost property, you have to deposit it with the police (else they seize the material), until the issue is resolved in court. Take the advice of security officers regarding this.
  4. IF YOU LOSE YOUR INSTITUTE ID-Card, do not directly complain to the police. Complain to the security section. They will compile all such complaints over a period, and report to the police. Meanwhile, they will issue a Duplicate ID card after due process, fines, etc.
  5. IF YOU HAD ALREADY REPORTED ABOUT LOST ID-Card AND GOT A DUPLICATE ISSUED, BUT FOUND YOUR ORIGINAL ID-Card after a while, please deposit the duplicate to the security section as soon as possible. If you are found with two ID cards,, you are liable to face the Disciplinary Action Committee and face a minimum punishment of semester suspension.
  6. HOSTEL ENTRY POLICY: Male/female students may enter and retain female/male guests in their room between 7 AM to 10 PM on all days barring special circumstances. However, when asked, the guest shall produce a valid identification and state the purpose of the visit. If your guest/guests is/are found in your room apart from the allotted timings, you are liable to face the Disciplinary Action Committee.
